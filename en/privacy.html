Alive Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026 | Effective Date: March 7, 2026

AFCMNZ ("we", "our") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Apple User Identifier (for account login and authentication)
  • Pair Code (for establishing 1-on-1 connections)
  • Daily check-in records (ignition time and streak days)
  • Device timezone information (for accurate daily check-in calculation)

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core pairing and spark features
  • To calculate and display consecutive check-in streaks
  • To sync check-in status between partners
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored on secure cloud servers.
  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Information Sharing

  • We do not share your personal information with any third party except your paired partner.
  • Your partner can only see your check-in status, not other personal information.

5. Your Rights

  • You can view your check-in records and pairing status at any time.
  • You can unbind from your partner at any time.
  • You can delete your account at any time. All data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. If we learn that we have collected information from a child, we will promptly delete it.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes within the App.
